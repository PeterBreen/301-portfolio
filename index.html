About Me

Hi, I’m Peter.

I do front-end development in Seattle, take photos (of airplanes, mostly), and hike mountains from time to time. I used to think I wanted to be an archivist. Now I think about making usable websites.

Chances are, if an airplane flies overhead, I can either identify it or look it up. It’s not like I have a frequently-used app just to find out just what model, carrier, and flight it is or anything.

I always feel that “about me” pages are like icebreakers at social events, so for the record: Puffins are my favorite breakfast cereal. Feel free to contact me below about airplanes, cereal, or work. Whatever suits you.
